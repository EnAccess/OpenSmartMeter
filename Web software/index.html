What we Do

EnAccess funds open source solutions for energy access. We fund two types of projects: Innovation Pilots and “Moonshot” concepts.

Innovation projects create hardware, software, or operational toolkits that companies around the world can use and immediately benefit from. Moonshot Concepts are open-ended research projects that promote new ideas in the industry.
